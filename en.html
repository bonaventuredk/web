SQP Project

Sequential Quadratic Programming - Matlab, trajectory optimization (Ariane)

November 2024 B. Dohemeto

Development of an SQP algorithm tested on several examples, including a stage configuration problem inspired by the Ariane launcher. We designed a simplified trajectory simulator and combined stage configuration optimization with trajectory optimization to define the most suitable launcher configuration for a given mission (mass 1250 kg, altitude 200 km, speed 7784 m/s). Implementation in Matlab.

3D Modeling & Meshing

Building, propeller and tower - Salome, sensitivity analysis (wind, earthquake)

February 2025 B. Dohemeto

3D building modeled and meshed with Salome: Geometry and meshing with/without refinement. Analysis of wind impact on maximum deformation, including extreme wind cases. Seismic study of the building and sensitivity analysis (wind and earthquakes).
3D propeller (in progress): Creation of a complete 3D propeller with 5 curved blades, adapted meshing for future simulation (forces, vibrations).
AON Center Tower: Geometry of a 332m tower (inspired by Chicago's AON Center), coarse mesh without refinement (in progress).
